💸 How to Save Money in College

Be smart with your spending — your future self will thank you!

📊

Budget Your Expenses

Create a monthly budget to track your spending on essentials and fun.

🍽️

Cook Your Own Meals

Save money by avoiding daily takeout — meal prep once, eat all week.

🎓

Use Student Discounts

Whether it’s Spotify, Amazon, or the local café — flash that ID!

🛒

Avoid Impulse Buys

Pause before purchasing. Ask: “Do I need this or want it?”

👯‍♂️

Split Costs With Friends

Share groceries, rides, and even subscriptions to lighten your load.

🌱 Start small. Save big. Your wallet will thank you!
